# Smartbox Content Engine — Product Summary

> *From a spreadsheet of products to a library of brand-ready video scripts, voiceovers, AI images, and social copy — in seconds.*

---

## What We Built

The **Smartbox Content Engine** is an AI-powered content automation platform purpose-built for Smartbox Group's product catalogue. A marketing or content team member uploads a standard product CSV file — the same data already in your product database — and within minutes receives a complete, brand-aligned content pack for every product row: a video script, voiceover copy, product description, a real AI-generated product image, a branded unboxing video clip, social hashtags, and a DAM-structured filename.

No brief writing. No agency back-and-forth. No inconsistent brand voice across markets. Just structured data in, polished creative assets out.

---

## The Problem It Solves

| Challenge | Before | After |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Content volume | 1 copywriter = ~10 products/day | 1 CSV upload = entire catalogue in minutes |
| Brand consistency | Varies by writer, market, deadline | Encoded once in the system prompt — always on-brand |
| Media production | Days of briefing, shooting, editing | AI-generated image + video per product, instantly |
| DAM organisation | Manual file naming, prone to errors | `PROD-{id}_{CATEGORY}_{LOCALE}_{DATE}.mp4` — auto-generated |
| Cost | Agency fees or large in-house team | Compute costs only — fractions of a cent per product |

## What It Generates Per Product

| Asset | Format | Details |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Video script** | Text | 30-second script, ~75 words, captures a mid-experience candid moment with vivid sensory detail |
| **Voiceover copy** | Text | 40–60 words, written for the ear, conversational warm-friend tone, gentle call to action |
| **Product description** | Text | 2–3 sentences, 40–60 words, evergreen (no pricing or seasonal references), focuses on feeling |
| **AI product image** | JPEG 16:9 | Photorealistic, generated by Google Vertex AI Imagen 4 Fast, natural lighting, real setting |
| **Branded video clip** | MP4 4 sec | Smartbox box-opening sequence → experience reveal, generated by Google Vertex AI Veo 3.1 Fast |
| **Image prompt** | Text | 60–80 word reusable creative brief for your photography team or image AI tools |
| **Video prompt** | Text | 15–25 word scene description, reusable for future video briefs |
| **Social hashtags** | Text | 5–8 lowercase hashtags, no `#` symbol, platform-ready |
| **DAM filename** | String | `PROD-{id}_{CATEGORY}_{LOCALE}_{YYYYMMDD}.mp4` — e.g. `PROD-1002_GETAWAYS_IE_20260317.mp4` |

## Brand Alignment — Built Into the AI

Brand consistency isn't enforced through review gates — it's **encoded into the AI's instructions** before it writes a single word.

### What the system prompt encodes

- **Prohibited words** — `embark`, `journey`, `unforgettable`, `indulge`, `luxurious`, `amazing`, `incredible`, `perfect gift` — words that feel generic or clichéd are blocked
- **Brand platform** — "Choose Wisely" — every script reinforces the idea that one well-chosen experience can change everything
- **Second-person warmth** — always "you", never cold or transactional
- **Evergreen copy** — no pricing, no dates, no seasonal references that would require re-generation
- **Field-level specs** — each output field has precise word counts, structural guidance, and tone constraints

### Per-category tone tuning

| Category | Creative Direction |
|----------|--------------------|
| Getaways | Quietly romantic, escapist — fresh sheets, open windows, intimate grounded joy |
| Wellness | Calm, restorative — gently humorous (the half-asleep massage smile), earned bliss |
| Adventure | Energetic, irreverently fun — celebrate the absurdity and adrenaline, punchy and gritty |
| Gastronomy | Sensory and warm — almost taste it, ritual and anticipation, sophisticated not snobbish |
| Pampering | Curious, infectious — nervousness before, the glow after, specific and vivid |

### Video template — the Smartbox moment

Every generated video clip opens with the same brand signature: a drone approaching a Smartbox-branded gift box, the lid opening with a soft glow, revealing the experience inside. This ensures every video asset starts with a consistent brand moment before showing the product-specific scene.

## Scale & Performance

- **Parallel processing** — up to 4 product rows processed simultaneously (configurable via `ROW_CONCURRENCY` environment variable)
- **Real-time feedback** — Server-Sent Events stream each step (text done → image done → video done) directly to the browser as they complete — no polling, no waiting for the full batch
- **A 50-row CSV** completes in approximately 3–5 minutes, depending on video provider quota
- **Two GCP projects** can be configured for load balancing — distributes calls randomly across both, doubling the available API quota
